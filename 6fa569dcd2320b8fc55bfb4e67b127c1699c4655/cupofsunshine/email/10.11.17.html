Early morning on October 2, 2017 Ireceived more messages than normal. Not knowing what was happening, I quickly checked the news at 3:30 a.m to discover the horrible tragedy. By now I'm sure you have heard something about the Las Vegas shooting, otherwise known as the 273rd mass shooting this year and the deadliest shooting in modern U.S. history.

I would like to highlight the inspiring things that were born from this incident.

  • Mike McGarry threw himself on top of his children as shots rang out. "They're 20. I'm 53. I lived a good life".
  • Jacob Soboroff joined Chris Hayes on MSNBC with four friends who survived Vegas attack. One, a firefighter, applied belts as tourniquets and pulled people out of the line of fire to save people's lives.
  • Sonny Melton, a registered nurse from Tennessee, tragically died saving his wife's life.
  • A GoFundMe campaign for Las Vegas victims has raised more than $2.9 million.
  • Las Vegas residents lit candles for shooting victims.
